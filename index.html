Mwit Birth Stat Calculator

Enter your mweors' levels, points, and heritage points and click 'Calculate Stats!' to see how many stats their mwit will be born with. Keep in mind mwit stats are calculated at birth, so if you continue to spar the parents during the pregnancy, the mwit could be born with more points than what was calculated here! A mweor's level and points can be found on its 'Sparring Info' page, and heritage points can be found in the lineage tab.
